Earth Day at Spring Harbor

Description:

Each year on Earth Day, the students, teachers and community of Spring Harbor Middle School spends the day working outside to improve the local environment. Projects including tending the gardens, working in the greenhouse, pulling invasive species and other work. We provide all the tools, safety equipment and healthy snacks. Please join us!
